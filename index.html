About Me

Jesse Salmon graduated from DeVry in 1989. He started working as a debug tech for Sun Microsystem after graduation. Where he first learned Unix command langauges, i.e. c-shell, korn shell. He also started training at California Karate Academy. In the years that followed Jesse moved to several different companies learning programming along the way. Jesse has worked for IBM, Applied Materials, Physio Systems, and Oracle. During the years Jesse also acheive the rank of 3rd degree Black Belt in shotokan karate from California Karate Academy
